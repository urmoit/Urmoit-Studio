Mining Inc 2! Map Revamp News

December 31, 2024

Big changes are coming to *Mining Inc 2!* Read on for all the exciting details about the upcoming map revamp!

🌍 A Bigger, Better Map!

The current map in *Mining Inc 2!* is about to get a massive upgrade! The old map, which was just one baseplate in size, will soon be replaced with a new map that's a whopping 8 baseplates—making it eight times bigger!

Even though the game will still feature one main mining area, the extra space will be packed with new zones to explore and exciting features to discover.

🎁 Hidden Secrets Await!

To make exploring even more fun, the revamped map will include secret locations scattered across the world. These hidden spots will reward players with free money and other helpful bonuses. Can you find them all?

🚧 Stay Tuned for Updates

This is just the beginning! We’re hard at work bringing this exciting update to life, and we can’t wait to share more. Keep an eye out for the First Leak, coming soon!

🎆🎇 Happy New Year! 🎆🎇

As we step into 2025, we want to take a moment to thank all of our amazing players and supporters. Your enthusiasm for *Mining Inc 2!* and the studio’s projects motivates us to keep pushing forward. We’re excited to bring you even more updates, and the best is yet to come!

Here's to a year of more mining adventures, new secrets, and even bigger updates. Stay tuned, and we wish you all a fantastic year ahead!
